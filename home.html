About Me

Hello! I'm Steve Wareham, a student of computer programming and graphic design. I'm passionate about creating intuitive user experiences and visually compelling designs that tell a story.

For over 20 years, I navigated the challenging and dynamic world of retail banking management, where I honed my skills in client relations, team leadership, and strategic planning. My journey from banking to tech and design is driven by my desire to blend analytical thinking with creative expression - and help small businesses narrow the "Tech Gap" that exists between them and mega corporations.

My background in banking has equipped me with a unique perspective on user needs and the importance of functionality, driving me to create designs and applications that are not only aesthetically pleasing but also user-friendly and efficient for small businesses.

One of my favorite things about web design and coding is the ability for me to utilize my skill set to enhance my hobbies. I'm an avid gamer and have extensive experience modding games like Grand Theft Auto 5, Baldur's Gate 3, and -of course- Skyrim. One of the unique skill sets I have picked up recently is modding VR games for Meta Quest optimization - which is a unique and immersive experience all it's own.

I've also been a fan of professional wrestling for over forty years, and have contributed to numerous blog sites and podcasts as a commentator and analyst. One of my favorite hobbies is playing WWE 2k24 and trying to make each match as interesting to watch as possible. You can check out one of my matches right here on my YouTube feed!
